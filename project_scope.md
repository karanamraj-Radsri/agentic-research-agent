# Project Scope for Research Agent

## Project Scope
The Research Agent project aims to develop an intelligent assistant capable of analyzing, organizing, and suggesting research papers in various domains. The objective is to simplify literature reviews and enhance research efficiency.

### Goals
- Implement a system that automatically gathers research papers from various sources.
- Analyze and categorize papers based on relevance and quality.
- Provide suggestions on related works and gaps in research.

### Deliverables
- A functioning prototype of the Research Agent.
- Documentation of the architecture and user manual.
- A report on user feedback and areas for improvement.
